# Executive Summary 

This project analyzes tech + finance datasets to evaluate revenue growth, profitability, customer performance, and risk indicators. The goal is to identify measurable trends that support business decisions such as pricing, retention strategy, and cost management.

## KPIs Tracked
- Revenue growth (MoM / QoQ)
- Profit margin and cost-to-revenue ratio
- ARPU and churn rate
- Volatility and expense spikes as risk signals

## Next Steps
- Load a public dataset (Kaggle/SEC-style financials + customer transactions)
- Standardize schema and create processed tables
- Generate visuals and finalize insights + recommendations
